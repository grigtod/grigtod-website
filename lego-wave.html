Lego Wave

Interactive installation for the LEGO Fifth Avenue NYC Flagship Store.
I helped Pixel Artworks with interactive screens that allow you to wave at Lady Liberty. When you wave long enough she waves back at you and plays one of many fun animations. You can see the wave interaction at 0:18 seconds in the video above.
This installation uses Azure Kinect Developer Kit to detect player interactions. The alembic animations are all rendered in real-time.
