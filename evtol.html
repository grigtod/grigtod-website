VX4

A project for Atlantic Productions who partnered with global aerospace and technology company Vertical Aerospace to bring their pioneering fully electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft to life on Apple Vision Pro. The revolutionary new aircraft, the VX4, aims to take travel to new heights – making journeys safer, cleaner and quieter. The interactive experience brings a previously unseen model of the aircraft to life at unprecedented high resolution, enabling an in depth look at the aircraft, and the industry leading technology that powers it.
